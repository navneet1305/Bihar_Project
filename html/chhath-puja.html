Chhath Puja

Four Days of Sunrise Devotion

Chhath is Bihar's most revered festival, honoring the Sun God with fasting, sacred river rituals, and offerings at dawn and dusk.

Ritual Highlights

  • Nahay Khay (ritual bath)
  • Kharna (fasting and prasad)
  • Sandhya Arghya at sunset
  • Usha Arghya at sunrise

Best Time to Visit

Late October to early November when the ghats glow with lamps and chants.

Where to Experience

Patna ghats, Ganga riverbank towns, and local ponds across Bihar.

Traveler Tips

Arrive early, wear modest attire, and keep pathways clear for devotees.

A Festival of Light and Discipline

Chhath Puja is defined by its purity and collective spirit. Families gather before sunrise, carrying woven baskets of fruits and sweets, offering gratitude to the Sun for life and renewal.
