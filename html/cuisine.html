TASTING TRAIL

Plan Your Food Journey

Three routes to explore Bihar's flavors based on season and mood.

Morning Market Crawl

Start with jalebi and chai, pick up seasonal vegetables, and end with a plate of litti.

Festive Sweet Run

Visit local sweet makers for thekua, anarsa, and warm khaja straight from the pan.

Evening Street Bites

Try ghugni, chana chaat, and sizzling tikkis on the riverside ghats.
