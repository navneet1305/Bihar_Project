Sonepur Mela

The Great Rural Fair of Bihar

Held at the confluence of the Ganga and Gandak, Sonepur Mela blends faith, trade, folk art, and celebration on a grand scale.

What to See

  • Cattle and craft bazaars
  • Folk music and performances
  • Ancient temples and rituals
  • Local food stalls

Best Time to Visit

November, during Kartik Purnima, when the fair is at its peak.

Where to Experience

Sonepur town near Patna, at the river confluence.

Traveler Tips

Visit early morning for rituals and return in the evening for performances.
