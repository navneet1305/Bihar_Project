FAQ

Need Quick Answers?

Here are the most common questions travelers ask before planning their Bihar journey.

How early should I book a guided trip?

We recommend booking at least 2-3 weeks in advance, especially during festival seasons.

Can you customize an itinerary for families?

Yes. We tailor routes based on age groups, mobility, and interest areas like heritage, food, or festivals.

Do you provide transport and accommodation?

We can suggest trusted local partners for transport and stays based on your budget and style.

What is the best season to visit Bihar?

October to March offers pleasant weather and the most festival activity.
