Buddha Purnima

The Day of Enlightenment

Buddha Purnima honors the birth, enlightenment, and nirvana of Lord Buddha. Bihar's sacred sites host serene prayers and gatherings.

Ritual Highlights

  • Peaceful prayers at monasteries
  • Candle and lamp offerings
  • Teachings and meditation
  • Community service

Best Time to Visit

April to May when sacred towns like Bodh Gaya are at their most vibrant.

Where to Experience

Bodh Gaya, Rajgir, Nalanda, and nearby monasteries.

Traveler Tips

Wear modest clothing, keep silence in prayer areas, and join guided walks.

A Day of Peace and Reflection

The festival reminds visitors of Bihar's spiritual heritage and the timeless message of compassion taught by the Buddha.
