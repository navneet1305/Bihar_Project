THE LEGACY

Where History Breathes

Bihar is not merely a state; it is a timeline of human evolution. From the ancient kingdom of Magadha which ruled most of South Asia, to the world's first university at Nalanda.

“Here, Prince Siddhartha became the Buddha. Here, Lord Mahavira attained Nirvana. Here, the tenth Sikh Guru was born.”

Today, it stands as a testament to resilience, offering travelers a blend of chaotic charm and profound peace.
